## Context

Two data files were computed under an **old label scheme** and never uploaded:

- `~/claude/ShimCurve/data/models/lmfdb_shim_models.txt` — 462 model records over 48 discriminants (6…462). No header; pipe-delimited; equations may contain embedded newlines, so records span multiple physical lines. Labels look like `26.1-[1]`, `26.1-[1,26]`, `94.1-[1,2]`.
- `~/claude/ShimCurve/data/rational points/lmfdb_shim_rational_pt_updated.txt` — ~424 records over 30 discriminants. 20 pipe-delimited columns, mostly `\N`; curve label in column 12; count of rational points in column 3 (`0`,…,`10`,`infinite`); coordinates in column 6.

Current curve labels in `gps_shimura_test` look like `6.1.1.4.0.a.1` = `{order_label}.{deg_mu}.{level}.{index}.{genus}.{class}.{num}` (order_label is `D` for maximal orders, `D.discO` for Eichler, giving 7- or 8-component labels; regexes in `~/claude/lmfdb/lmfdb/shimura_curves/main.py:58-62`).

Working hypothesis (confirm per Q1): old label = `D.N-[m1,...]` = quotient of X₀(D;N) by the Atkin–Lehner subgroup generated by the w_m listed (so `[1]` = trivial quotient = X₀(D;N) itself).

## Task

Produce a validated mapping table `old_label → new_label` (or `old_label → UNMAPPED/reason`) covering every distinct label in both files, plus a short report on the label grammar.

## Steps

1. Extract all distinct old labels from both files (beware multi-line model records — a label field always matches `^\d+\.\d+-\[[0-9,]*\]$`).
2. Confirm/refute the grammar hypothesis empirically, without waiting on Q1 if possible:
   - For several `[1]` labels, compare the model's evident genus (conic in 3 vars ⇒ g0; y² − quartic ⇒ g1…; hyperelliptic of degree 6 ⇒ g2, etc.) against the genus of X₀(D;N) from the devmirror: `select label, genus from gps_shimura_test where "discB"=D and "discO"=D*N and deg_mu=1 and level=1;`
   - For quotient labels, compare against `SignatureX0DNmodAtkinLehnerElement` (in `~/claude/ShimCurve/code/tables/signatures_single_AL_element_X0DN.m`) or the genus tables of X₀(D;N)/⟨w_m⟩ computed via `X0DN_code.m`.
3. For `[1]` labels: map to the unique devmirror row with `"discB"=D, "discO"=D*N, deg_mu=1, level=1`. Record the found label. Flag any (D,N) with no row (means the coarse table doesn't cover it) or multiple rows.
4. For proper-quotient labels: these have **no rows today** except for D=6 (where the 5 level-1 curves `genera-D6-deg1-N1.m` are exactly X₀(6;1) and its AL quotients). For D=6, match quotients to those rows by genus + AL data (`autmuO_norms` column holds the squarefree norms of the Aut-generators — a quotient by ⟨w_m⟩ should have m in its norms). For other D, emit `UNMAPPED: no target row yet (needs T19/T20 coverage or decision Q1.2)`.
5. Write the map as `~/claude/lmfdb/shimcurve_tickets/artifacts/T01-label-map.csv` (`old_label,new_label,status,notes`), and a `T01-report.md` beside it describing the grammar, the validation evidence, and counts (mapped / unmapped by reason).

## Acceptance criteria

- Every distinct label in both files appears exactly once in the CSV.
- ≥ 3 genus cross-checks documented for `[1]` labels and ≥ 2 for quotient labels (or an explanation of why impossible).
- No guessed mappings: anything not validated is UNMAPPED with a reason.
